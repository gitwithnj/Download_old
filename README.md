# Shreeram Wood Interiors - Premium Furniture & Design Website

A beautiful, modern website for interior design and premium wooden furniture with editable contact information.

## Features

- **Premium Design**: Elegant, modern interface with wooden furniture theme
- **Editable Contact Information**: Click on any contact field to edit directly in the browser
- **Auto-Save**: All contact information changes are automatically saved to browser localStorage
- **Responsive Design**: Works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Smooth Animations**: Beautiful scroll animations and hover effects
- **Contact Form**: Functional contact form for customer inquiries

## How to Run

Simply open `index.html` in your web browser:

1. **Double-click** on `index.html`, or
2. **Right-click** on `index.html` → Open With → Your preferred browser, or
3. **Drag and drop** `index.html` into your browser window

No server or installation required! The website runs completely locally in your browser.

## Editing Contact Information

1. Navigate to the **Contact** section
2. Click on any contact field (Address, Phone, Email, Business Hours, or Website)
3. Edit the text directly
4. Click away or press Enter to save
5. Your changes are automatically saved and will persist when you reload the page

## Sections

- **Home**: Hero section with call-to-action
- **About**: Company information and statistics
- **Gallery**: Showcase of interior design projects
- **Furniture**: Premium furniture collections
- **Contact**: Editable contact information and contact form
